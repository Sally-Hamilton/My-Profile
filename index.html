My Design Process

A systematic approach to creating effective learning experiences

1

Analyze

Identify learning needs, audience characteristics, and performance gaps through stakeholder interviews and needs analysis.

2

Design

Define learning objectives, create assessments, and design the instructional strategy aligned with business goals.

3

Develop

Create storyboards, develop content, and build interactive learning materials using appropriate authoring tools.

4

Implement

Deploy learning solutions, train facilitators, and ensure smooth rollout across the organization.

5

Evaluate

Measure learning effectiveness, gather feedback, and continuously improve based on data and learner insights.
